The Challenge is to design Battery Charger for Lithium Ion with customable features
include various indicators & alert system. 3cell Lithium Ion battery was used in this project.
This project consist of two main blocks 1) SMPS 2) Battery Charing & Controlling Unit.
A Microcontroller was use to control the battery charging. My work was focus on controlling Unit.
Its Hardware & Firmware desgning along with its PCB. Alpha version of this product was tested successfully.
