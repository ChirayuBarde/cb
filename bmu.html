The Challenge is to design a Battery Charger for Lithium Ion Battery with customable features
including various indicators and alert systems. Three cell Lithium Ion battery was used in this project.
This project consists of two main blocks
1) SMPS- Switching Mode Power Supply
2) Battery Charging & Controlling Unit.
A Microcontroller was used to control the battery charging. The work was focused on controlling Unit.
Its hardware, firmware and PCBs were designed by me. Alpha version of this product was tested successfully.
