Audio Synthesizer using APR33a3 is a mini project completed in my 3rd year of engineering.
I was searching for a device that could record our voice and save it without using any controller and memorycard. So I conducted a research and found APR33a3.
Which is a wonderful IC, that not only could record one, but 8 different voices upto 1.5 minutes each.
So I decided to work on this project. The first step was to make the project on breadboard & then convert it to the final project on PCB.
I used Proteus software & etched PCB at home. A good audio quality was achieved. The project was really fun.
